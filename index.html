Know Us Better

InAmigos Foundation was founded on September 23, 2020, by Govind Shukla with a mission to create significant and lasting social impact. InAmigos Foundation is a 8 non-profit organization registered under the Government of India and located in Chhattisgarh, providing services to communities using compassion, innovation, and collective actions. With 80G and 12A accreditation, the foundation ensures that all activities carried out are transparent and accountable, as well as offering incentives to donors through the provision of tax relief. Additionally, InAmigos Foundation is CSR-1 accredited and therefore can partner with corporates to carry out CSR projects. Besides, the foundation has NITI Aayog registration and is also certified according to IAF ISO 9001:2015, which indicates its commitment to quality management.

Our Projects:

• Project Seva Ensuring that needy people have food, clothes, and other necessities.

• Project Bachpanshala Improving access to learning and educational opportunities among disadvantaged children.

• Project Jeev Animal rescue, protection, medical care, and feeding programs.

• Project Udaan Helping women develop skills for empowerment and entrepreneurship.

• Project Prakriti Creating sustainability and improving awareness of environmental issues.

• Project Vikas Vocational skills training to improve employability and livelihood.

Our Commitment:

Backed up by an enthusiastic team of volunteers, professionals, donors, and corporations, we endeavor to bring about enduring positive changes within different communities. We interact with our supporters through our activities and online presence, letting them know about the results of our endeavors.

InAmigos Foundation believes that significant changes can be brought about only when people unite for a common cause. All contributions help in advancing causes like education, food for the hungry, empowerment of women, animals, environment, and community development within India.

Our Projects

We are involved in various projects aimed at improving the quality of life for underprivileged communities. From educational programs to healthcare initiatives, our projects are designed to create lasting impact.

➢ Project SEVA – Over 50,000+ meals and clothing distributed to the underprivileged.

➢ Project BACHPANSHALA – Bridging educational gaps by training underprivileged children in basic digital literacy, life skills, and school education support.

➢ Project JEEV – Dedicated to animal welfare, feeding 50+ stray animals daily through our volunteer network.

➢ Project UDAAN– Empowering women by collaborating with self-help groups in rural areas, promoting financial independence, skill development, and menstrual hygiene awareness.

➢ Project PRAKRITI– Advocating for sustainability and environmental conservation by planting 20,000+ saplings and supporting eco-friendly agriculture.

➢ Project VIKAS – Facilitating employment and skill development through internships in various fields, having trained 30,000+ interns in the last four years in data operations, finance, research, content writing, digital marketing, social work, and more.

At InAmigos Foundation, we believe in the power of collective action. Our transparent operations and strong partnerships enable us to bring real, measurable change to society. Through our social media presence and online platforms, we ensure that every initiative reaches the right audience, encouraging more individuals and organizations to join our mission.
